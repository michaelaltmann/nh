Distribution of Nursing Homes in Minnesota

This page is an experimental visualization of the availablility of nursing homes across Minnesota. The map on the left shows the distribution of nursing homes. For comparison, the map on the right shows the spatial distribution of grocery stores. You can hover your mouse over a nursing home to see the average number of kilometers traveled by people in that nursing home's service area. {{> main}}
